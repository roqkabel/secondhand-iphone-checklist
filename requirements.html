Requirments


  • (Optional) a place with a WiFi Network
  • Another smartphone to share Network and/or to test connectivity
  • A sim card with 4G/5G data plan
  • The tool to plug/unplug the sim card
  • A Bluetooth device to test Bluetooth connectivity (AirPods/ Apple Watch are preferred)
  • An external battery to test the charging input
  • (Optional) a lightning cable or ask the vendor for the cable
  • An Apple account to log in with it on the new iPhone
