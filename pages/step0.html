Step 0: Cosmetic checks


Check the back and front of the iPhone.
Check if there are no significant scratches (+ your cosmetic criteria acceptance)

Check the edges of the iPhone.
Check if there are no significant scratches (+ your cosmetic criteria acceptance)

Check that the screen or the battery compartment are well glued.
Check that there are no gaps

Check that there are no scratches on the cameras.
Check that there are no scratches (+ your cosmetic criteria acceptance)

Test all the buttons.
You shouldn't feel any undesired pressure.

If the iPhone is already On, Switch it Off, then Switch it On
You should be the person who switches on the iPhone.
