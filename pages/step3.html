Step 3: Check the iPhone IMEI


Go to settings → General → About → Copy the number in IMEI

Copy and paste the IMEI to this website imeicheck.com/imei-check to check if it was stolen or blacklisted by any country.

The GSMA Blacklist Status should be Clean

Check IMEI

If the iPhone is clean, you can go to step 4
