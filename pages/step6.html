Step 6: Network checking

In this step, you need a second phone/ a zone with a WiFi


Share the WiFi from your first phone / Connect to the WiFi you are located in and test the internet
If you can navigate with Safari, the test is a success

How to Connect to Wi-Fi on your iPhone

Before removing your SIM CARD from your old phone, check how many network bars you have

Put your SIM CARD and check how many network bars you have on the iPhone
If you have the same or (minus one) bar, the test is a success

Make a phone call
If you can call and the other person is hearing you, the test is a success

Use your SIM DATA (4G/5G) and test the internet with it (do not forget to disable WiFi)
If you can navigate with Safari, the test is a success
