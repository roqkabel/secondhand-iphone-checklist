Step 1: Check your iPhone Model


Go to settings → General → About → Retreive the number in Model Number

If the Model Number starts with
M This iPhone is new
P This iPhone is personalized by Apple
N This iPhone is an official replacement by Apple
F This iPhone is an official refurbish by Apple
Else This iPhone may not be official. We advise you to avoid it.
